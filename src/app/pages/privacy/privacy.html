Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 18, 2026

Your privacy is vital to us. This Privacy Policy details how Smart Travel Planers collect, store, utilize, and protect your information when utilizing our smart travel solutions.

1. Information We Collect

To orchestrate customized travel experiences, we collect elements including:

  • Account Credentials: Your full name, email address, password, or Gmail login identification.
  • Travel Preferences: Budget limits, dates, preferred style indicators, and interactive chat text prompts.
  • Local Caching Data: Your email address stored via local storage when "Remember Me" is activated.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data strictly to refine planning tools and provide requested services:

  • Synthesizing and routing itineraries through our backend Semantic Kernel engine.
  • Generating PDF files of your schedule and rendering leaflet interactive router maps.
  • Sending password retrieval alerts and confirming account status changes.

3. Caching and Storage Security

If you check "Remember me on this device", we store your email inside your browser's `localStorage` to expedite login actions. We utilize secure encryption protocols on the backend to protect your profile parameters. However, no database transaction is 100% secure, and we advise caution.

4. Third-Party Solvers & APIs

We integrate open mapping layers (Leaflet / OpenStreetMap), photo search catalogs (Unsplash), and AI semantic models. These external modules operate under separate privacy frameworks, and we encourage you to review their specific policies.

5. Your Controls

You can request account deletion, clear cached credentials from your local storage at any time, or modify your travel styles dashboard. Your preferences are adjustable directly in your settings layout.
